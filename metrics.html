The "Success" ‚Äî Efficiency Metrics vs. Customer Satisfaction

If you say "Accuracy," I will send you back to the drawing board. Accuracy is a model metric, not a product metric. We measure business value.

The Metrics Trap

Junior PMs fall in love with "accuracy" because it's what the ML team talks about. But accuracy is an internal metric‚Äîit tells engineers how well the model is performing, not whether the product is delivering value.

A model with 99% accuracy that nobody uses is worthless. A model with 85% accuracy that saves $2M annually is a success.

üöó Parallel Example

Uber Support

Uber doesn't measure "Did the AI understand the rider?" That's too abstract. They measure concrete outcomes: "Did we resolve the ticket in fewer interactions?" and "Did the rider book another ride within 7 days?" Business metrics, not model metrics.

‚ö†Ô∏è The Accuracy Trap

"Our model has 94% accuracy" means nothing in a board meeting. The CFO wants to know: "How much money did this save us?" and "Are customers happier or angrier?" Always translate model performance into business impact.

Which metric proves we saved the company money?

üí° Why AHT (Average Handling Time)?

AHT is the North Star because it directly translates to labor cost. If we reduce AHT from 8 minutes to 5 minutes per ticket, and we handle 10,000 tickets/month, that's 500 fewer agent-hours needed. At $25/hour loaded cost, that's $12,500/month in savings. AHT also correlates with customer satisfaction‚Äîfaster resolution = happier customers.

The Metrics Hierarchy

Not all metrics are equal. Build a hierarchy that tells a complete story:

North Star (Business): Average Handling Time ‚Üí Directly tied to labor cost and customer satisfaction.

Leading Indicator: Auto-classification Rate ‚Üí What % of emails are routed without human intervention? Leading indicator of AHT reduction.

Quality Guard: Reroute Rate ‚Üí If this spikes, the model is breaking. Quality safeguard.

Sanity Check: CSAT Score ‚Üí Are customers actually happier? If AHT drops but CSAT drops too, we're cutting corners.
