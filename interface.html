The "Interface" ‚Äî UX for the Support Agent

The user is the Support Agent. They are stressed and working fast. The AI should not slow them down‚Äîit should make them faster.

The User We're Designing For

Forget the customer for a moment. Our primary user is the Customer Support Agent (CSA). They're handling 50+ tickets a day, working against an SLA timer, and one bad interaction away from a negative Glassdoor review.

The AI needs to be invisible when it's right and obvious when it needs help. This is not a consumer app‚Äîit's a productivity tool. Every click counts.

Gmail Smart Reply

Google doesn't force you to use AI-generated replies. It suggests 3 options at the bottom. You can ignore them, tap one, or type your own. It saves typing time without taking control away from the user. The AI is a suggestion, not a decision-maker.
